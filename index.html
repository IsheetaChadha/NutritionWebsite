NutriSnap

Your food, decoded.

NutriSnap is a professional-grade tool that links physical products to global nutrition databases to give you lab-accurate data and cost tracking.

How to use

1
Scan BarcodePoint your camera at any food product's barcode to identify it instantly.
2
Analyze DataView 10+ precision data points fetched from Open Food Facts and USDA records.
3
Save to StashAdd items to your "Stash" to keep a running total of your nutrition and spending.

The Stash

The Stash is your local digital grocery bag. It calculates your total estimated costs and saves your recently scanned items so you don't lose your data.
